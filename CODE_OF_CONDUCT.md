# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, welcoming community that values clarity, calm collaboration, and long-lived work.

## Expectations

* Be respectful and constructive in all discussions.
* Assume good intent; ask for clarification before judging.
* Keep feedback actionable and tied to project goals.
* Prioritize accessibility, performance, and simplicity in decisions.

## Unacceptable behavior

* Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
* Disruptive trolling or repeated off-topic comments.
* Sharing private information without consent.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue in the relevant repository or contact a Code Particles maintainer. Reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainership may take any action deemed appropriate, including warnings, issue locking, or removal from the community. Decisions prioritize the safety and longevity of the projects.
